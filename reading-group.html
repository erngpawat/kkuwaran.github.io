(Thailand) Data Science Reading Group

This group is a data science reading group only for Engineering Students in Chulalongkorn University, coordinated by Kananart Kuwaranancharoen. The purpose of the group is to provide a platform for sharing knowledge and intellectual discussions on any topics in data science. We aim to serve a larger community in the near future. Please wait for the announcement.

If you are engineering students in Chulalongkorn University and interested in joining this group, please contact me directly.

Topics including but not limited to:

  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • Statistics
  • Data Mining
  • Data Visualization

Current Format

The reading group consists of a weekly meeting and a mailing list.
